Understanding Climate change


🌍 Climate change means long term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These changes are mostly caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and cutting down forests. 🔥 As a result, we're seeing more heatwaves, floods, and rising sea levels. 🌱 But there's hope if we learn, act, and protect our planet, we can create a better future.
